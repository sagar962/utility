About Free Online Utility Services

Convert scanned PDF and images to editable text

OnlineOCR.net is a free web-based Optical Character Recognition software (OCR) that allows you to convert scanned PDF documents (including multipage files), faxes, photographs or digital camera captured images into editable and searchable electronic documents including Adobe PDF, Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, Rtf, Html and Txt. If you have a scanned document and want to avoid retyping your document, Free Online OCR service is the fast way to do it

Multilingual conversion

OnlineOCR.net supports 46 languages including Western and Asian such as English, Danish, Dutch, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Spanish, Japanese, Chinese, Korean and others. Our service allows to process documents which are written in more than one language.

Free service

Free Online OCR is a free service in a "Guest mode" (without registration) that allows you to convert 15 images per hour.
